# Sewer ABC Smoke
## Description
In CS2 a single smoke grenade is enough to block off both the left and right sides of ABC due to volumetric smoke spread, this smoke has become critically important for a good execute because of it.

**Difficulty:** 2/5
**Importance:** 5/5
